# Method: Save the Cat! (Blake Snyder)

> **Category:** Structure
> **Load when:** `intent.methods_preference.structure` enthält `save-the-cat`

## §0 Konzept

15-Beat-Struktur, kommerziell-orientiert (originally Drehbuch). Sehr präzise
Positionen, geeignet für genre-konforme Romane (Thriller, Romance, Adventure).

## §1 Die 15 Beats (kanonisch positioniert)

| # | Beat | Position (von 100%) | Funktion |
|---|------|---------------------|----------|
| 1 | **Opening Image** | 0% | Setting establishment |
| 2 | **Theme Stated** | 5% | Thematic question subtle |
| 3 | **Set-Up** | 1-10% | World, Characters |
| 4 | **Catalyst** | 10% | Inciting Incident |
| 5 | **Debate** | 10-20% | Refusal/Doubt |
| 6 | **Break Into Two** | 20% | Akt I → II |
| 7 | **B Story** | 22% | Sub-Plot (often RS) |
| 8 | **Fun and Games** | 20-50% | Genre-Promise delivered |
| 9 | **Midpoint** | 50% | Plot reversal or false victory |
| 10 | **Bad Guys Close In** | 50-75% | Antagonist gains power |
| 11 | **All Is Lost** | 75% | Lowest point |
| 12 | **Dark Night of the Soul** | 75-80% | Internal despair |
| 13 | **Break Into Three** | 80% | Akt II → III |
| 14 | **Finale** | 80-99% | Climax + resolution |
| 15 | **Final Image** | 100% | Mirror to Opening |

## §2 Pro 40-Kapitel-Roman

| Beat | Kapitel-Position (von 40) |
|------|---------------------------|
| Catalyst | Kap 4 |
| Break Into Two | Kap 8 |
| Midpoint | Kap 20 |
| All Is Lost | Kap 30 |
| Break Into Three | Kap 32 |
| Finale | Kap 33-39 |
| Final Image | Kap 40 |

## §3 Hard Rules

- **Beats sind positions-kritisch** — wenn Midpoint zu Kap 15, ist es nicht mehr Save-the-Cat
- **Theme Stated muss vor Catalyst sein** — sonst wirkt das Theme aufgesetzt
- **B Story ist obligatorisch** — selbst wenn klein
